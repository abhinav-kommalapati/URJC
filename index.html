Undergraduate Research Journal Club

Empowering UMass Amherst students to showcase their research, connect across disciplines, and contribute to academic excellence through peer-reviewed publication.

Join the Club 🚀 Learn More 📖

Our Mission

We provide a platform for undergraduate students across all departments at UMass Amherst to showcase their research work. Our club collects, edits, and publishes a student-led research journal in digital format, promoting student research and making it accessible to peers, professors, and the broader UMass community.

What We Do

📝

Collect & Curate Research

We maintain a dedicated submission website where students can submit their original research papers for peer review and potential publication.

✏️

Editorial Excellence

Our editorial board works collaboratively to review, edit, and compile high-quality student research into professional journal issues.

🌐

Digital Publishing

We publish journal issues online and promote them through email campaigns and social media to maximize research visibility.

🎓

Workshops & Training

Regular workshops on research writing, publishing ethics, data visualization, and academic presentation skills.

🤝

Faculty Connections

We facilitate opportunities for faculty feedback, mentorship, and recognition of outstanding student research work.

🔬

Cross-Disciplinary Collaboration

Connect with researchers from different departments and explore interdisciplinary approaches to complex problems.

What You'll Gain

📊

Research Experience

Gain hands-on experience with the academic publishing process from submission to final publication.

👥

Peer Review Skills

Learn to review and edit research papers, developing critical thinking and constructive feedback abilities.

🎯

Leadership Opportunities

Take on editorial board roles, manage projects, and develop practical leadership and management skills.

🌟

Portfolio Building

Build a strong academic portfolio with published work and editorial experience for graduate school or career applications.

💡

Interdisciplinary Learning

Collaborate with students from different fields and gain exposure to diverse research methodologies.

🔗

Professional Network

Connect with faculty mentors, peer researchers, and build relationships that support your academic journey.

Five-Year Vision

2025-2026

Launch our first journal issue and establish our website and submission system infrastructure.

2026-2027

Double our submission volume and establish partnerships with 3+ academic departments across campus.

2027-2028

Launch our print edition pilot program and establish a faculty advisory board for enhanced guidance.

2028-2029

Host our first campus-wide undergraduate research symposium to celebrate student achievements.

2029-2030

Archive 5 complete journal volumes and gain recognition as an official UMass research outlet.
