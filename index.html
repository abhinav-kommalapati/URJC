Our Mission

📌 The Club

The mission of the UMass Undergraduate Research Journal Club (URJC) is to provide students with opportunities to develop their research writing skills through workshops and information sessions, while also offering a selective platform for exceptional undergraduate work to be peer-reviewed and published in its affiliated journal, The Flagship. The club also fosters academic and professional connections through networking with affiliated faculty and a semesterly symposium.

📰 The Flagship Undergraduate Research Journal

The Flagship Undergraduate Research Journal at UMass Amherst is committed to publishing and showcasing exceptional undergraduate research papers and articles across a wide range of disciplines within the Five College Consortium. The journal aims to provide a platform for students to present original, well-supported research that is comprehensible to a broader audience.

Published by UMass Undergraduates

The Flagship is a student-led research journal accepting original work across all disciplines — humanities, social sciences, STEM, and beyond. Submissions are open to students across the Five College Consortium.

Each issue is peer-reviewed by fellow students and faculty advisors, then published online and promoted across campus to maximize your research's reach.

What We Do

📝

Collect & Curate Research

We maintain a dedicated submission website where students can submit their original research papers for peer review and potential publication in The Flagship.

✏️

Peer Review & Editing

Our editorial board collaboratively reviews, edits, and compiles high-quality student research. Each paper receives feedback from both student editors and professor advisors.

🚩

Publish Through The Flagship

We publish journal issues through The Flagship URJ — our affiliated undergraduate research journal — and promote each issue via email campaigns and social media.

🎓

Workshops & Info Sessions

Regular workshops on research writing and publishing ethics help students improve their academic communication and prepare competitive submissions.

🤝

Faculty Connections

We facilitate opportunities for faculty feedback, mentorship, and recognition of outstanding student research work across all departments.

🔬

Cross-Disciplinary Collaboration

Connect with researchers from different departments across the Five College Consortium and explore interdisciplinary approaches to complex problems.

Submission Process

1

Submit Your Research

Fill out our submission form with your paper, department, and research details. Open to all UMass Amherst and Five College undergraduate students.

2

Initial Editorial Review

The editorial board performs an initial screening to assess scope, originality, and formatting. Authors are notified within 2 weeks.

3

Peer Review

Accepted submissions are reviewed by fellow student editors and faculty advisor reviewers from the relevant department.

4

Revisions & Feedback

Authors receive detailed feedback and may be asked to revise their paper. This collaborative process ensures publication-ready quality.

5

Final Editing & Compilation

Approved papers are copy-edited and formatted for inclusion in the next issue of The Flagship.

6

Publication & Promotion

Your research is published online in The Flagship and promoted via email and social media to the UMass and Five College community.
